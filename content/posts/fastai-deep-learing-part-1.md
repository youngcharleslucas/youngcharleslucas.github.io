## getting started  

What is *deep learning*? 
Deep learning is an artificial intelligence (AI) methodthat teaches computers to process data 
in a way inspired by the human brain. Deep learning models can recognize complex pictures, 
text, sounds, and other data patterns to produce accurate insights and predictions.
