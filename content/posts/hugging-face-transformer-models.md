Difference between NLP and LLM?  

**Natural Language Processing**: 
- broader field  
- enable computers to understand, interpret, and generate human language  
- techniques include: sentiment analysis, named entity recognition, machine translation  

**Large Language Models**: 
- subset of NLP models  
- massive size and extensive training data 
- perform a wide range of language tasks with minimal task-specific training
